A small idea

Every other map app asks the same question: what's fastest? Yander asks a different one — what's worth seeing on the way? A back lane through the Cotswolds. A coast road you'd never have found. A pub at the end of a green dot you didn't know existed.

We're building the routing engine for the journey, not the arrival.

What Yander does differently

Routing with a point of view.

Scenic by default

Routes optimised for the view — not the motorway. Pull in viewpoints, villages, woodland, coast, and the kind of B-roads locals know about. Always with an honest ETA.

Drive mode, with voice

Turn-by-turn navigation built for the back-road brief. Calm voice prompts, big legible glances, background location that survives a phone call. A co-pilot, not a chaperone.

Yander Free, properly free

A genuine 50-mile sandbox. Plan, save, navigate — no trial timer, no dark patterns. When you're ready for unlimited range, GPX, offline tiles and Ordnance Survey detail, Yander Premium is one tap away.

Mapping you can trust

The good data. Properly licensed.

Yander is built on the same foundations the AA and OS App lean on. Open data for the world; Ordnance Survey detail for Britain; routing engines we host ourselves so your journey isn't telemetry for somebody else.

  • OpenStreetMap

    Worldwide base map — millions of contributors, openly licensed under ODbL.

  • Ordnance Survey

    OS Data Hub for Britain — bridleways, byways, Crown-copyright detail no consumer map matches.

  • OSRM & Valhalla

    Self-hosted routing engines — your route is calculated on infrastructure we run, not sold on as data.

  • Wikimedia

    Wikipedia and Commons for places, photos and stories you actually want to read on the road.
